About Mr. Keenth Smith The Proprietor

Mr. Keenth Smith founded Brain's High School on 10th of Septemeber 2001. Mr. Keenth Smith was born in Nigeria and a native of Akpugeze, Oji River in Enugu State of Nigeria. He is a London based Lawer and a human right activist. He is the founder of the a human rights non-governmental orgaisation.
