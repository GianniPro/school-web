Excellent Results (External Exams)

We use this as a medium to appreciate and encourage the parent/guardians and the stuents who performed excellently well in their external examinations. As the percentage number of students with 5 credits and above including English and Mathematics in 2022/2023 academic session are 98.5% in WEAC, 92.35% in NECO and 98.9% in BECE which eventully earned us an award from the Federal Ministry of Education as the college produced the best performing student in BECE and WEAC 2022/2023 in all schools. These and many more are encouraging signs that our students are doing excellently well.

Academics

We concluded all academic programmes scheduled for the term as planned and the first term results will be accessible online as usual and hard copies will be issued to them on resumption.
